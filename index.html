About Me

I'm an AI researcher and engineer with a passion for advancing the field of artificial intelligence. With seven years of experience at Meta AI, I've developed deep expertise in Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Multi-Agent Systems.

Currently, I'm working at Pokee AI, where I continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in AI research and applications. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions to complex problems using cutting-edge AI techniques.

Throughout my career, I've been dedicated to exploring the structure and dynamics of multi-agent reinforcement learning systems, contributing to both theoretical advancements and practical applications in the field.
